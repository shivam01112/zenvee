About Zenvee
  • What is Zenvee?
    Zenvee is a premium manufacturer and supplier of hotel toiletries and dry amenities, offering thoughtfully curated products that combine hygiene, aesthetics, and sustainability. Established in 2016, Zenvee has become a trusted name in India’s hospitality industry.
  • Who owns Zenvee?
    Zenvee is a brand under QUALSOL, specializing in premium hotel amenities. The company is led by a dedicated team with years of expertise in hospitality solutions.
  • Where is Zenvee based?
    Zenvee is based in Noida, India, with its office located at B115, G.T. Karnal Road, Industrial Area, Delhi 110033. We proudly serve hotels, resorts, guesthouses, and boutique stays across India.
  • What products does Zenvee offer?
    We offer a complete range of hotel toiletries such as shampoos, body wash, lotions, and soaps, along with premium dry amenities including dental kits, shaving kits, combs, vanity kits, sanitary bags, shoe shine kits, and shower caps.
  • Do you provide customized branding and packaging?
    Yes. Zenvee has its own in-house printing unit, allowing us to create fully customized labeling and packaging solutions. We can add your hotel’s logo, name, or unique message to enhance your brand identity while maintaining consistent quality and fast delivery.
  • What makes Zenvee different?
    Zenvee stands out for its reliability, customization, and attention to detail. With over 9 years of experience, we focus on creating premium, eco-friendly, and aesthetically pleasing amenities that leave lasting impressions on your guests.
